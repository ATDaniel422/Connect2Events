About C2E

Whether you live in the midst of a bustling metropolis, or in a small town deep in the mountains, we all need a place to find whatever it is we're looking for.

From political rallies, marathons, farmers markets, or an impormput game of Frisbee, C2E offers the best place to find events in your local community. All events are user generated, and most come from verified users so you can feel safe attending an event you find here.

Browse events in your area, create an account to recieve alerts and add events to your own calendar, or create an event yourself. Always free, always safe, and always conecting communities, C2E is the place people go to find places to go
